    Welcome to Tokyo, Japan. A group of teens have been spotted running around in costumes calling themselves 'The Phantom Thieves of Hearts'. Apparently, they steal the hearts of those corrupt men and women who hold positions of power. How do they do it? With a mysterious power known only as a 'Persona'. Will the path they take lead to glory? Or ruin? Find out this Valentine's Day.

    THE TEAM

    JOKER


    REAL NAME: UNKNOWN


    The Protagonist. Second student year in Shuujin High School. His role in a mysterious violent incident causes him to be forcibly transfered to the school for juvenile delinquents. Aided by the power of the Persona, he and his 'companions' traverse The Palace, the realm that exists within the hearts of all humans. His Persona is Arsene, based on Arsene Lupin, the infamous Victorian thief and dandy.

    SKULL


    REAL NAME: RYUJI SAKAMOTO


    Second year student in Shuujin High School. He shines as a student athlete, but his past is filled with pain and abuse. He joins up with our Hero in order to get back at the adults that he feels have wronged him all throughout his life. His Persona is Captain Kidd, based on the infamous looting and plundering privateer.

    PANTHER


    REAL NAME: ANNE TAKAMAKI


    Second year in Shuujin High School. She's a foreign student, whose half-Japanese, half-European appearance causes contention and discomfort among her peers. No one quite knows her reason for joining The Phantom Thieves; she is known to keep to herself often. Her Persona is Carmen, based on the globetrotting femme fatale.
